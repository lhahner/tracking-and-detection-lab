ndjdj## Abstract
Here are datasets that are open to use with their content as of setting, type of data e.g. Radar or LIDAR and further accessibility and size. It might be helpful for any segmentation, detection and tracking task.
Most of the datasets out there only cover videos from high-angle, respectively this list should also cover all the different viewing-angles; high-angle, low-angle, bird's-eye, close-up, wide shot, and Dutch angle.
Another good overview is provided by https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_datasets_in_computer_vision_and_image_processing#Aerial_images.

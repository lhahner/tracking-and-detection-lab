A review on multi-object tracking systems was done by many researches in the past.
Considering different aspects and introducing various categorization. This section
offers some insights in those. Li et al \ref{a-review-of-mot-in-recent-times} from 2025
gives a very recent overview of the current state of the art. They identify essential
technologies in the field of MOT. They categorize MOT 
in detection-based, SOT-based and segmentationn based.
Further they highlight the key challeneges of MOT currantly as of the 
dependency of the underlying detection system. Furthermore crowded scenes
are still hard to handle for modern trackinst system. Finally the quality of training data
should be optimal to gain the beste results.
